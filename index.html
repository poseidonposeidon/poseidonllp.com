Intro

We are an investment research organization focused on value investing and long-term planning, committed to growing together with our investment targets. We believe that only through long-term capital investment and cooperation can enterprises truly achieve their growth goals. This belief drives us to focus on enterprises with long-term growth potential, strong competitive advantages, and undervalued evaluations.

Poseidon Capital's investment strategy revolves around three core principles. First, we seek enterprises with robust growth potential, which typically possess innovation capabilities, extensive market coverage, leading technologies, and high-barrier moats. Second, we emphasize the operational capabilities of enterprises, including brand influence, excellent management teams, and financial decision-making abilities.

Our core competitiveness comes from continuous learning and research capabilities. Our company has an experienced, highly professional, and industry-savvy investment research team. We delve into the market, conducting comprehensive analyses of industry trends, enterprise financial conditions, and competitive landscapes to provide a basis for investment decisions. We also provide strategic advice and financial support when necessary to help enterprises achieve long-term growth goals.

We will continue to uphold our principles and grow together with our invested enterprises and partners.
